## Ingredients 

* 2 large beetroot - cooked 
* 1/2 tsp cumin 
* 1/2 tsp ground coriander
* 3 cloves of garlic 
* Pinch of salt 
* 300ml greek yogurt 
* 1/2 lemon juiced 

## Method 

1. Put it in a blender and blend it! 


## Comments 

All the above ingredients are a rough estimation. Add bits at a time and enjoy the ride.
