This is a recipe from my grandmas sister Ruby. 

## Ingredients

* 1kg green tomatoes
* 500g white onion
* 500g apples
* 25g salt
* 8oz raisons 
* 4oz sultanas
* 1 litre malt vinegar
* 350g demera sugar


## Method 

1. Slice or mince tomatoes, apples, onions
2. Add raisins, salt, vinegar
3. Simmer for 90 minutes
4. Add sugar
5. Cook until thick
