## Stew Ingredients 

* 1-2 tbsp peanut oil
* 500g sweet potato - peeled and diced
* 1 x 400g tin of black-eyed peas, drained and rinsed / or black beans 
* 1 x green chilli
* 3 tbsp tomato puree
* 1 x 400g tin of chopped tomatoes
* 500ml vegetable stock 
* 125g smooth peanut butter 
* 200g spinach 
* 1 tbsp lemon juice 
* Handful of corriander
* 2 spring onions 

## Paste Ingredients 

* 2 onions - roughly chopped 
* 5 garlic cloves - roughly chopped
* 2 inch piece of ginger - roughly chopped
* 1 tsp paprika 
* 2 tsp ground coriander
* 1 tsp ground turmeric 
* 2 tsp cumin 
* 1 tsp ground fenugreek 
* 1 green chilli

## Method 

1. Blitz paste ingredients 
2. Add oil to pan and saute paste for 10 mins on a low/medium heat
3. Add sweet potato, black eyed pas/beans, 1/2 green chilli, and tomato puree. Combine 
4. Add tinned tomatoes, vegetable stock, peanut butter. Season. Cover with a lid and simmer for 25 mins 
5. Remove from heat and add spinach. Leave spinach to wilt for 5 minutes. 
6. serve with coriander, spring onions, lemon juice and fresh chilli if wanted.
